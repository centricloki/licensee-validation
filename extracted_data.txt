The tobacco distributor licenses are good for only one year, or from the issue date to the end of the year, so they all expire 12/31.  They could cancel the license before the expiration date.  If we enter a cancel date, the system automatically updates and it would no longer show on this list.  
Steps to Fetch Data: To find tobacco distributors that have an active Tobacco Distributor’s license, please do the following:

1)	Go to the website www.ksrevenue.gov
2)	On orange strip, hover mouse over Taxation, select Business Tax
3)	Select blue box Business Tax Forms in lower right-hand corner of screen
4)	Under Forms and Instructions, select the 3rd bullet point Cigarette and Tobacco Licensing and Tax Information
5)	About 1/3 of the way down the page, below Cigarette/Tobacco Registrations, there are some bullet points, some are indented, select the link that is back to the left Active Cigarette, Consumable Material, and Tobacco Licensee Database
6)	Select the blue box in the lower left had corner Click here to access our database.
7)	Under License Type, there is a drop-down menu that defaults to Cigarette Retailer, select the downward arrow the select Tobacco Distributor.   
8)	You can narrow your search by selecting one or more of the following, Kansas County, City Name, and/or State.
9)	In the blue box and in the lower left-hand corner of the screen, select Search.  This will bring up all certificates that are licensed in the State of Kansas based on your search criteria.  If you do not select any search criteria, it will bring up all active certificates.
